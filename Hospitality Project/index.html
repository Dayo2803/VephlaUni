Rediscover Sydney with our Love Sydney package. With complimentary self-parking, daily breakfast for two and late check-out till 1.00 p.m., there’s so much more time to relax and explore Sydney from the heart of the city.

 

Package Privileges:

  • Daily breakfast for up to two persons at The Place
  • Self-parking for one vehicle at Secure Parking**
  • Complimentary late check-out till 1.00 p.m.

Customise your stay with the following Fullerton Experiences^: 

  • Enjoy our Signature Afternoon Tea Experience with a glass of Veuve Cliquot Yellow Label Brut NV champagne at The Bar, for an additional A$156 for two persons  

 

Terms and Conditions:

  • **Secure Parking is located at the entrance of 159 Pitt Street. Entitled to entry and exit for one vehicle per 24-hour period. Tickets must be validated at the hotel Concierge Desk. Any additional hours will be charged at a rate equal to a full nightly/24-hour rate.
